NIE INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY


An educational institution is a temple of learning for all age groups where people gain knowledge ethically. They provide a large variety of learning environments and learning spaces. Education is any act or experience that has a formative effect on the mind, character, or physical ability of an individual.

NIE Institute of Technology, affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU)and approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), is one of the foremost institutions initiated by NIE foundation (R).

The institution was established in the year 2008 and is located in prominent industrial area of Mysuru. The premier industries such as BEML, Infosys technologies, Kluber Lubricants, Cyient DLM, Automotive Axles, Vinyas Technologies, Skanray Technologies, Kaynes Technologies, Rane Madras Ltd. SAN Engineering and locomotive co. Ltd. other medium and small scale industries/enterprises are situated nearby the institute. This aids the students and faculty members to have effective industry-institute interactions and collaborative support leading to learning, research, discovery and innovation.

  • To encourage entrepreneurial skill and innovations in emerging technologies
  • To inculcate professional ethics and leadership qualities to address the societal needs.
